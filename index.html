WellMind AI – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 25, 2025

WellMind AI (“we,” “our,” or “us”) provides the WellMind Student Wellness App (“the App”) to support student well-being, mindfulness, and mental health awareness in educational environments.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, analytics data, device identifiers, or user content. No login data, usage statistics, or personally identifiable information (PII) is sent to any external servers or third-party services.

2. Use of Data

Since the App does not collect any data, it does not analyze, use, or share any information for analytics, marketing, or personalization purposes.

3. Data Sharing

The App does not share any information with external entities, advertising networks, analytics providers, or any third-party organizations.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is designed for use by students of all ages and complies with COPPA and other international privacy standards protecting children’s data.

5. App Permissions

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Location services
  • Contacts
  • Photo library
  • Files or storage

The App does not access these features. Any permission used in future updates will be solely for core functionality and disclosed clearly before activation.

6. Security

Although no personal data is collected, the App follows standard mobile-app security guidelines to ensure safe and secure usage for all students and educators.

7. Changes to This Policy

If future versions of the App introduce data collection or online features, this Privacy Policy will be updated, and a new version will be published on this page with the effective date clearly indicated.
