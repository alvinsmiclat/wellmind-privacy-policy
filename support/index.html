Quick Troubleshooting

  • App won’t open / crashes: restart your device, ensure iOS/iPadOS is up to date, then reinstall the app.
  • No sound or haptics: check Silent Mode and sound settings; verify any Focus modes.
  • Animations not visible: ensure Reduce Motion is disabled (Settings → Accessibility → Motion).
  • Slow performance: close background apps and reboot the device.

When contacting us, include:

  • App: WellMind – Student Wellness
  • App version (Settings → About inside the app, if available)
  • Device model & iOS/iPadOS version (e.g., iPhone 14, iOS 18.0)
  • Exact steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots or a short screen recording (if possible)
